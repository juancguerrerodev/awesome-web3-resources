# Layer 2 Solutions and Scaling

Comprehensive guide to Ethereum Layer 2 solutions for scaling and reducing transaction costs.

## What are Layer 2 Solutions?

Layer 2 solutions are blockchain systems that operate on top of Ethereum mainnet (Layer 1) to increase transaction throughput and reduce gas fees while maintaining security.

## Types of Layer 2 Solutions

### Optimistic Rollups

Optimistic rollups assume transactions are valid by default and only run computation when challenged.

- **Optimism** - https://optimism.io - EVM-compatible optimistic rollup
- **Arbitrum** - https://arbitrum.io - Leading optimistic rollup with multiple chains
- **Base** - https://base.org - Coinbase-backed optimistic rollup

### Zero-Knowledge Rollups

ZK-rollups use cryptographic proofs to validate transactions off-chain.

- **zkSync** - https://zksync.io - High-performance ZK rollup
- **StarkNet** - https://starknet.io - Cairo-based ZK rollup
- **Polygon zkEVM** - https://polygon.technology/zkEVM - EVM-compatible ZK rollup

### Sidechains

Independent blockchains connected to Ethereum with their own security model.

- **Polygon (Matic)** - https://polygon.technology - Most popular sidechain
- **Gnosis Chain** - https://gnosis.io - Stable and reliable sidechain

### Payment Channels

- **Lightning Network** - For Bitcoin transactions
- **Raiden Network** - Ethereum payment channels

## Getting Started with Layer 2

1. **Choose a Layer 2** - Select based on your needs (speed, cost, ecosystem)
2. **Set up Wallet** - Configure MetaMask or other wallet for Layer 2 RPC
3. **Bridge Assets** - Transfer ETH and tokens from mainnet
4. **Deploy/Interact** - Use DApps on Layer 2

## Cost Comparison

- **Ethereum Mainnet** - Higher gas fees (100+ gwei)
- **Optimism** - ~50-90% savings on gas
- **Arbitrum** - ~50-70% savings on gas
- **zkSync** - ~85-95% savings on gas
- **Polygon** - ~99% savings on gas

## Security Considerations

- Layer 2s inherit security from Ethereum
- Use official bridge contracts
- Verify contract addresses
- Be aware of sequencer risks
- Monitor community discussions
