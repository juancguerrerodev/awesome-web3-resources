# NFT Guides and Resources

A comprehensive guide to NFTs, digital assets, and decentralized art platforms.

## What are NFTs?

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique digital assets built on blockchain technology, representing ownership of digital art, collectibles, and more.

## Popular NFT Marketplaces

- **OpenSea** - https://opensea.io - The largest NFT marketplace
- **Blur** - https://blur.io - Creator-friendly NFT marketplace
- **Rarible** - https://rarible.com - Community-driven NFT platform
- **SuperRare** - https://superrare.com - Premium digital art marketplace
- **Foundation** - https://foundation.app - Curated NFT platform
- **Magic Eden** - https://magiceden.io - Solana NFT marketplace

## NFT Standards

- **ERC-721** - Standard for non-fungible tokens on Ethereum
- **ERC-1155** - Multi-token standard for both fungible and non-fungible tokens
- **SPL** - Solana Program Library tokens
- **Metaplex** - Solana's NFT standard

## Smart Contract Development

- **Hardhat** - Ethereum development environment
- **Truffle** - Framework for Ethereum smart contracts
- **OpenZeppelin** - Library for secure smart contract development
- **thirdweb** - Web3 development tools for NFTs

## NFT Security Best Practices

- Never share your seed phrase or private keys
- Use hardware wallets for long-term storage
- Verify contract addresses before interacting
- Beware of phishing scams and fake projects
- Use reputable platforms and services

## Gas Optimization

- Batch mint operations to reduce gas costs
- Use Layer 2 solutions for cheaper transactions
- Optimize contract code for efficiency
